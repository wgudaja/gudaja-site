# GUDAJA — image prompts & art direction

Goal: replace the weak vector motifs with a small set of high-end, cinematic images that match the site. Discreet distribution-intelligence tone, not corporate stock.

## Art direction (applies to every image)

- Palette: near-black and deep charcoal, cool neutral greys, clean white highlights. Exactly one restrained red accent per image (a single distant light, reflection or signal). Never saturated, never spread across the frame.
- Lighting: low-key, single or rim light, soft falloff, some atmospheric haze. Most of the frame in shadow.
- Composition: strong negative space for text. Off-centre subject. Serious, quiet, expensive.
- Do NOT include: people or faces, brand logos, readable text, flags, luxury clichés (cars, watches), HDR over-processing, bright or multi-colour palettes.
- Output: high resolution (2400px+ on the long edge). Leave a darker area (left side for hero, lower third for bands) so white text stays legible.
- After you generate or shoot them, send them over. I will grade them to match (deepen blacks, desaturate, unify the red, add fine grain) and place them.

Midjourney: append the aspect flag and `--style raw --v 6`. Flux / DALL·E / Ideogram: drop the `--` flags and state the aspect in words.
